Top Tips for Financial Wellness

  • 🧾 Track Every Rupee: Record even small expenses — they add up quickly.
  • 📊 Plan Monthly: Split your budget into Needs, Wants, and Savings.
  • 🛍️ Delay Impulse Buys: Use the 24-hour rule for non-essentials.
  • 💸 Automate Savings: Auto-debit money to a savings/investment account.
  • 💳 Use UPI & Cards Wisely: Stick to your digital budget like it's cash.
  • 🧠 Weekly Reviews: Reflect on your spending every Sunday night.
  • 🎯 Set Clear Goals: Save with a purpose — visualize your future.
  • 📉 Cut Subscriptions: Cancel anything unused or underutilized.
