Smart Spending & Saving Tips

💡 “Do not save what is left after spending, but spend what is left after saving.”
— Warren Buffett

  • 🧾 Track Every Rupee: Record even small expenses — it adds up.
  • 📊 Plan Monthly: Allocate your budget across needs, wants, and savings.
  • 🛍️ Delay Impulse Buys: Use the 24-hour rule for non-essentials.
  • 💸 Automate Savings: Set up auto-debits to a savings account.
  • 💳 Use UPI & Cards Wisely: Treat digital money like physical cash.
  • 🧠 Weekly Reviews: Reflect on spending every Sunday night.
  • 🎯 Set Goals: Visualize what you're saving for — it builds discipline.
  • 📉 Cut Subscriptions: Audit and cancel unused services.

📘 "A budget is telling your money where to go, instead of wondering where it went."
— Dave Ramsey
